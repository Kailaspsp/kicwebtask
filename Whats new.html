360 INVICTUS for India from India

Meet the BELL-360 INVICTUS . Specifically configured for the Indian Air Force, the BELL-360 INVICTUS provides unmatched ‘Make in India’ opportunities and strengthens India’s path to an advanced airpower.The BELL-360 INVICTUS addresses the Indian Air Force’s unique requirements and integrates India into the world’s largest military helicopter ecosystem with the world’s pre-eminent defence company. Aerone and Tata would produce the BELL-360 INVICTUS in India, for India.




Advanced Medium Combact Helicopter (AMCH)

The AMCA program evolved out of the Medium Combat Helicopter (MCA) programme,will several requirements for a common fighter to replace different types of existing Helicopter which included Mi 24 and 28.Incorporating stealth features AMCH is a twin-engined Helicopter which is powered by two GTRE K 9 + or K 10 engine which are successors to the GTRE Kaveri engine. internal weapons and sensors are mounted using a modular system, in accordance with air force's specifications, to anticipate future upgrades and obsolescence.
